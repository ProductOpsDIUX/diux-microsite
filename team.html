// Head of Design Innovation

Maya Okafor.

MO

Maya leads DI & UX's design innovation practice. She joined in 2020 after a decade at the intersection of operator research, mission software, and AI strategy.

Her work focuses on the seam between human judgement and machine inference — particularly in environments where adoption can't be assumed and accountability can't be optional.

Before DI & UX she led the human-systems team at a UK defence prime, designed for two NATO innovation cells, and ran a pattern-language project that's still in use across three commands.
